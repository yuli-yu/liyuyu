Charlie’s Adventures

1. 查理的新一天 | Charlie’s New Day

查理穿着条纹衬衫、吊带裤和圆形眼镜，笑容满面地从床上跳下来。他兴奋地说：“今天会是充满乐趣的一天！”

Charlie, wearing a striped shirt, suspenders, and round sunglasses, jumps out of bed with a big smile. He excitedly says, “Today is going to be a fun day!”

2. 查理在公园 | Charlie in the Park

查理来到了公园，他看到远处有一个小女孩正坐在秋千上，看起来有点孤单。查理走过去，笑着说：“嗨！我叫查理，想一起玩吗？”

Charlie arrives at the park and notices a little girl sitting alone on a swing, looking a bit lonely. Charlie walks over, smiling, and says, “Hi! I’m Charlie. Want to play with me?”

3. 新的朋友 | New Friends

小女孩笑了，点了点头：“你好！我叫小雪。”查理高兴地牵着她的手，带她去玩跳绳。

The little girl smiles and nods, “Hi! I’m Snow.” Charlie happily takes her hand and leads her to play jump rope.

4. 一起游戏 | Playing Together

查理和小雪开始玩跳绳，跳得又快又开心。查理笑着说：“跳绳真的好好玩！”

Charlie and Snow start jumping rope together, laughing and having fun. Charlie says, “Jump rope is so much fun!”

5. 伸出援手 | Lending a Hand

查理注意到一位老奶奶在公园长椅上休息，她的购物袋看起来很沉。查理走过去，轻声问：“奶奶，我可以帮您提一下袋子吗？”

Charlie notices an elderly lady sitting on a park bench, and her shopping bags look heavy. Charlie walks over and gently asks, “Can I help you with your bags, Grandma?”

6. 帮忙的小行动 | A Little Act of Help

查理帮助老奶奶提起购物袋，一起走到她的家门口。老奶奶感谢地说：“谢谢你，查理，真是个善良的孩子！”

Charlie helps the elderly lady carry her shopping bags, walking her to her doorstep. The lady thanks him, saying, “Thank you, Charlie. You’re such a kind boy!”

7. 查理和小雪的合作 | Charlie and Snow Working Together

查理和小雪一起开始搭建一个沙堡，他们一起动手堆沙子、挖沟，团队合作让他们完成了一个大沙堡。

Charlie and Snow start building a sandcastle together. They work as a team, piling up sand and digging trenches, and soon they have built a big sandcastle.

8. 分享零食 | Sharing Snacks

查理从背包里拿出自己喜欢的饼干，递给小雪：“你喜欢这些吗？一起吃吧！”小雪开心地接过零食：“谢谢你，查理！”

Charlie takes out his favorite cookies from his backpack and offers them to Snow, “Do you like these? Let’s share!” Snow happily takes the snack, “Thank you, Charlie!”

9. 帮助小动物 | Helping a Little Animal

查理和小雪看到一只小鸟飞到了低矮的树枝上，看起来有点困。他们小心翼翼地帮助小鸟飞走，查理说：“每一个生命都值得我们关心！”
